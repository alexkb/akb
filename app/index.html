AKB Web Services

AKB Web Services is a small business based in Perth, Western Australia and established in 2005 by Alex Bergin.

We can provide development, design and support services around web, mobile and cloud technologies using open source solutions and open standards.

Past projects

Stamp Duty Calculator AU mobile app 2015

This calcualtor helps you determine the stamp duty that might be due on property purchased in Australia. The application uses the hybrid mobile development framework: Ionic & AngualarJS. Check it out on Google Play or the App Store.

Agweb Intranet 2014

Agweb is the intranet site of the Dept Agriculture & Food, Western Australia. This department has over 1,000 employees across the state using the intranet for policies, news, tools, etc. The Agweb was built in Drupal using over 40 3rd party Drupal modules.
